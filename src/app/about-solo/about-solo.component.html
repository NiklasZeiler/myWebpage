My Story

Who am I?

My name is Niklas Zeiler and I am a Front-End Developer.

Why did I become a software developer?

The first time I developed something was at school when I created our graduation CD with PowerPoint.For me, becoming a software developer was the next logical step. After working in the car industry for seven years and becoming more and more interested in programming, I decided to turn my passion into a profession. Some time ago I started to read up on programming with the help of the literature and wrote my first small projects. I soon realized that I could do this with the help of professional support. When I became aware of the Developer Academy in 2021, I decided to start training there to become a software developer. During this training, I learned to write many projects alone or in teamwork. With the help of experienced software developers, I was able to reach a higher level.

What are my strengths?

My way of working is characterized by a lot of reliability, commitment, and independent work. Due to my previous work in a large car dealership, I can handle stress well and always find a solution. As a result, I also have a very high degree of initiative, which was additionally promoted by creating my projects. When working with several people, my ability to work in a team came in handy. My willingness to learn and my interest in solving new tasks are also among my strengths.

What are my skills?

During my further education, I learned various front-end technologies such as HTML5, CSS3 and JavaScript. The framework Angular and the associated TypeScript are other technologies that I can use. I can also handle techniques such as Responsive Web Design. With object orientation, I have programmed a small game. Thanks to experienced software developers, I was able to constantly improve my skills and find and fix errors in code.

What is my vision?

With my knowledge, I would like to develop technologies that help other people. Software that makes people's lives easier, at work and home.
