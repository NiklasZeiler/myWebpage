My Story

Who am I?

My name is Niklas Zeiler and I am a Front-End Developer.

What are my skills?

During my further education, I learned various front-end technologies such as HTML5, CSS3 and JavaScript. The framework Angular and the associated TypeScript are other technologies that I can use. I can also handle techniques such as Responsive Web Design. With object orientation, I have programmed a small game. Thanks to experienced software developers, I was able to constantly improve my skills and find and fix errors in code.

Why did I become a software developer?

For me, becoming a software developer was the next logical step. After working in the automotive industry for seven years and becoming more and more interested in programming, I decided to turn my passion into a profession. Some time ago, I started reading up on programming with the help of the literature and wrote my first small projects. Soon I realized that I could do this with professional support. When I became aware of the Developer Academy in 2021, I decided to train there to become a software developer. During this training, I built various projects from responsive websites to small business apps in a team or on my own.

What are my strengths?

Due to my previous work in a large car dealership, I can handle stress well and always find a solution. As a result, I also have a very high degree of initiative, which was additionally promoted by creating my projects. When working with several people, my ability to work in a team came in handy. My willingness to learn and my interest in solving new tasks are also among my strengths.
