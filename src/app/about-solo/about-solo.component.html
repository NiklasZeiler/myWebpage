My Story

Who am I?

My name is Niklas Zeiler and I am a Front-End Developer.

Why did I become a software developer?

For me, becoming a software developer was the next logical step. The first time I got involved with developing anything was in school when I created our graduation CD using PowerPoint. After working in my profession for seven years, but my interest in programming never waned, I decided to make my passion my profession. Some time ago I started to teach myself independently, with the help of literature. But without a teacher, this project was very difficult and I made only small progress. This changed in 2021, thanks to the Developer Academy I now had a teacher and could realize my wish to become a software developer. During my training, I was able to write many projects alone or in teamwork and I always received suggestions for improvement from experienced software developers, which helped me to quickly reach a higher level.

What are my strengths?

My way of working is characterized by a lot of reliability, commitment, and independent work. Due to my previous work in a large car dealership, I can handle stress well and always find a solution. As a result, I also have a very high degree of initiative, which was additionally promoted by creating my projects. When working with several people, my ability to work in a team came in handy. My willingness to learn and my interest in solving new tasks are also among my strengths.

What are my skills?

During my further education, I have learned different frontend technologies like HTML5, CSS3 or JavaScript, also I can handle techniques like Responsive Webdesign. With object orientation, I have programmed a small game. The framework Angular and related TypeScript is also no problem for me. Thanks to the experienced software developers I could improve my skills constantly and find and fix errors in my code quickly.

What is my vision?

With my knowledge, I would like to develop technologies that help other people. Software that makes people's lives easier, at work and home.
