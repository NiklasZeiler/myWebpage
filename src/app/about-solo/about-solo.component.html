My Story

Who am I?

My name is Niklas Zeiler and I am a frontend developer.

Why did I become a software developer?

For me it was the next logical step to become a software developer. The first time I had contact to develop something was in school when I created our graduation CD with PowerPoint. After working in my profession for seven years now, but my interest in programming didn't decrease, I decided to make my passion my profession. Some time ago I started to buy literature for independent learning and worked through it. But without a teacher this project was very difficult and I made only small progress. That changed in 2021, thanks to the Developer Academy I now had a teacher and could realize the desire to become a software developer.

What are my strengths?

My way of working is characterized by a lot of reliability, commitment and independent work. Due to my past work in a large car dealership, I can handle stress well and always find a solution. As a result, I also possess a very high degree of initiative. My willingness to learn and my interest in solving new tasks are also among my strengths.

What are my skills?

During my further education I have learned different frontend technologies like HTML5, CSS3 or JavaScript, also I can handle techniques like Responsive Webdesign. With object orientation I have programmed a small game. The framework Angular and related TypeScript are also no problem for me. Thanks to the experienced software developers I could improve my skills constantly and find and fix errors in my code quickly.

What is my vision?

With my knowledge I would like to develop technologies that help other people. Software that makes people's lives easier, at work and at home.
